The Continuum

Experience simplicity with The Continuum's superb location situated between the University of Florida campus and Downtown Gainesville. Life is hassle-free with all-inclusive pricing, cutting edge amenities, and a friendly staff ready to help you achieve a successful professional lifestyle. As the official graduate and professional housing partner of the University of Florida, we're with you every step of the way.

All buildings feature restricted access, elevators, and secure bicycle storage. Each apartment is fully furnished and comes with classic features such as faux wood flooring, stainless steel energy-efficient appliances, private bathrooms, in-unit washer and dryer, and spacious closets.

Amenities

We provide fully furnished apartment homes with classic features ranging from studios to four bedrooms with roommate matching available. You can rely on our caring and helpful staff to find you a convenient place to call home. Our goal is to provide you with a community that fulfills your needs as a student or staff member during your time at the University of Florida. Go Gators!
